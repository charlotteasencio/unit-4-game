Coffee Drinker

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. This is the exact amount of coffee you need to make it through the day.

There are four cups of coffee below. By clicking on a cup of coffee, you will add a specific amount of caffeine points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your caffeine points to the amount of coffee needed and therefore being properly caffeinated to survive the day.

You lose the game if your amount of caffeine goes above the total amount needed as you will become too hyper.

The amount of caffeine in each cup of coffee is hidden from you until you click on it and it is added to the total amount of caffeine consumed.

Every time the game restarts, the amount of caffeine in each cup of coffee will change.
