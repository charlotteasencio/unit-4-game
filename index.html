Crystal Collector

You will be given a an amount to collect at the start of the game. This is the exact value of crystals that you need to match.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will collect a specific amount of points to your total collected amount.

You win the game by matching your total collected amount to the amount to collect.

You lose the game if your total collected amount is more than the amount to collect.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it and it is added to the collected amount.

Every time the game restarts, the value of each crystal will change.
