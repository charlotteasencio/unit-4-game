Crystals Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the computer generated random number. You lose the game is your total score goes above the computer generated random number.

The value of each cyrstal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Every time the game restarts, the value of each crystal will change.
